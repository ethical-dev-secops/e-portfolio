Unit 10: Research Writing

In this Unit, we examined the utility of good research

Authors such as Huang (2010) examine what makes research useful and meaningful in the academic world. Other authors such as Dhillon (2022) explain more of the structure of a good article.

For this unit, the Summative Assessment consisted of a research proposal outline and presentation. My focus was aligned to the work of Mason (2019), Pratsri et al (2021, September) and Tulshan et al (2019) on how AI virtual assistants can be utilized to augment Security Automation and Monitoring.
