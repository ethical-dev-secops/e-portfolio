Unit 3: Research Methods

Quantitative and Qualitative methodologies were investigated in detail. Qualitative studies involve interviews with their benefits primarily focused on abstracts and subjective correlations (Tinker, 2008). Quantitative research primarily involves experiments and questionnaires, to draw statistical outcomes (Drijvers, 2018). The seminar involved exploring these research methods.
