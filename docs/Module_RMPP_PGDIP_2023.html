Conclusion - Review of Course as a Whole

I greatly benefited from the statistical work in this module. It will be of immense use in my Master’s Thesis. I am already planning on different research models for my surveys and interviews. I have learnt new research methodologies that I can put into practice and I feel this module will help me with my academic career ahead. This Module has provided me with the very necessary pre-requisites to tackle a Master’s Thesis. I feel confident in peer-reviewing research articles, and my knowledge of statistics has expanded. I am grateful to the course curators and designers for this well thought out curriculum.
