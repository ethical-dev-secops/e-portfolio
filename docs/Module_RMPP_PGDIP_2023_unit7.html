Unit 7: Validity and Generalisability in Research

This units topics mostly involved around:

  • Qualitative and Quantitative Research.
  • Ethics and Statistical Bias within Research
  • Summative Assessment: Literature Review

In unit 7 a collaborative learning discussion was conducted. I responded to three of my peers, and enquired deeper about ethical considerations. My investigation into research ethics was primarily dedicated on the foundational principles of Natural Law (Haskell, 1990; Spencer, 1893).

The recommended reading also delved into the depths of statistical bias and how bad survey techniques can interfere with random sampling (Kaplan, 2005).
